Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide pasta, savory meat sauce (typically beef or pork with tomatoes), and creamy béchamel sauce. Melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top create a golden-brown crust when baked. The result is a hearty, flavorful, and comforting meal that showcases the rich and harmonious blend of textures and tastes inherent in Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté finely chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until softened.
  3. Add ground meat, cook until browned, and drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
  5. In a separate saucepan, prepare béchamel sauce with butter, flour, and milk.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. In a baking dish, layer cooked noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and shredded mozzarella.
  8. Repeat layers, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  10. Allow lasagna to rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
